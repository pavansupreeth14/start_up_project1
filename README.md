    ABOUT Project


DevStack is a modern internship platform dedicated to helping students gain practical industry experience through real-time projects, guided learning, and skill-based training programs.

We offer 4-week internship programs in various technology domains such as Web Development, App Development, Artificial Intelligence, UI/UX Design, and more. Our internships are designed to help students improve their technical knowledge and build professional experience.

After successful registration, students receive an official Internship Offer Letter from DEVSTACK. During the internship period, participants are assigned weekly tasks and mini projects that help them develop practical problem-solving skills and hands-on experience.

Students are required to complete and submit all assigned tasks within the internship duration. After successful completion of the internship, DEVSTACK provides an Internship Completion Certificate as recognition of the student’s performance, dedication, and learning journey.

Our mission is to create a valuable learning environment where students can enhance their skills, gain confidence, and prepare themselves for future career opportunities in the technology industry.
